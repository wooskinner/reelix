Cookie Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

📌 Quick Summary: We use cookies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and understand how you use Reelix. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your actions and preferences over time.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential: To keep you signed in and make the service work
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and language
  • Analytics: To understand how you use Reelix and improve it
  • Security: To protect your account and prevent fraud

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and analytics
  • Google Analytics: Usage analytics
  • TMDB: Content metadata (no cookies set)
  • Selar: Payment processing (secure checkout)
  • Cloudflare: Security and performance

5. Your Cookie Choices

You can control cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
  • Cookie Banner: You can accept or decline non-essential cookies
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics cookies using Google's opt-out tool
