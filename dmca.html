DMCA Notice

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

📌 Quick Summary: Reelix respects intellectual property rights. If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed, submit a DMCA takedown notice to us.

1. Copyright Policy

Reelix respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. We comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and respond to valid takedown notices.

2. What We Do

Important: Reelix does not host any content on our servers. We only provide links to media hosted on third-party services. However, we still take copyright claims seriously and will investigate all valid DMCA notices.

3. Submitting a DMCA Notice

To file a DMCA takedown notice, please send an email to cinemanest@proton.me with the following information:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed, or if multiple works, a representative list.
  2. Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing, with enough detail (URLs, titles, etc.) for us to locate it.
  3. Your contact information including name, address, phone number, and email address.
  4. A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized by the copyright owner.
  5. A statement that the information in the notice is accurate, under penalty of perjury.
  6. Your physical or electronic signature (or that of someone authorized to act on your behalf).

📝 Sample DMCA Notice Template

To: Reelix DMCA Agent Email: cinemanest@proton.me Subject: DMCA Takedown Notice I, [Your Full Name], am the copyright owner of [describe your work]. The following material is infringing my copyright: [URL of the infringing content on Reelix] My contact information: Name: [Your Full Name] Address: [Your Address] Phone: [Your Phone Number] Email: [Your Email Address] I have a good faith belief that the use of the material is not authorized by me, the copyright owner. I declare under penalty of perjury that the information in this notice is accurate and that I am the copyright owner. Signature: [Your Signature or "/s/ Your Name"] Date: [Date]

4. Counter-Notification

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may file a counter-notification with the following information:

  • Your name, address, phone number, and email
  • Identification of the removed material
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed in error
  • Your consent to jurisdiction in the appropriate court
  • Your physical or electronic signature

5. Repeat Infringers

Reelix may terminate accounts of users who are repeat infringers of intellectual property rights.
