Get the app on your device.

Download APK
After downloading, open the file and allow "install from unknown sources" if prompted.

Install Reelix on iOS:

  1. Open reelix.2bd.net in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add in the top right
Reelix will now appear as an app icon on your home screen and open full-screen, just like a native app.
