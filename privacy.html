Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

📌 Quick Summary: We collect only what's needed to provide the service. We don't sell your data. Your watch history and preferences stay private. You can delete your data anytime.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address: For account creation and communication
  • Name: For personalization
  • Phone number: Optional, for account recovery
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely

1.2 Usage Information

  • Watch history: What you've watched and where you paused
  • Search queries: What you search for
  • My List: Movies and shows you've saved
  • Preferences: Your settings and preferences

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device information: Browser, OS, device type
  • IP address: For geolocation and security
  • Cookies: For session management and preferences
  • Analytics: Usage patterns to improve the service

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the service: Streaming, recommendations, watchlist
  • To improve the service: Analytics, A/B testing, feature development
  • To communicate: Account updates, service announcements, marketing (with consent)
  • For security: Fraud prevention, account protection
  • For legal compliance: As required by law

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contract: To provide the service you signed up for
  • Consent: When you agree to data processing
  • Legitimate interest: To improve the service and prevent fraud
  • Legal obligation: When required by law

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices:

  • Firebase: Google's secure cloud platform
  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access control: Only authorized personnel can access data
  • Regular audits: We review security practices regularly

5. Data Sharing

We share data with third-party services that help us operate:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics
  • Selar: Payment processing (payment data only)
  • TMDB: Content metadata (public data only)
  • Cloudflare: CDN and security

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we hold about you
  • Rectify: Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict: Limit how we use your data
  • Object: Object to data processing
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at cinemanest@proton.me.

7. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Authentication, session management
  • Preferences: Storing your settings
  • Analytics: Understanding how you use the service

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses.

9. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we'll delete your data within 30 days, unless required to keep it for legal reasons.

10. Children's Privacy

Reelix isn't intended for children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we'll delete it.

11. Third-Party Links

Our service may contain links to third-party websites. We're not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you via email or through the service. Continued use indicates acceptance of the new policy.

13. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us at cinemanest@proton.me.
